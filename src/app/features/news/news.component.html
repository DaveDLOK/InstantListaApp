Welcome to InstantLista

In this application you will be able to create your own lists and share them with your network, it's pretty easy

How can I create my lists?

You can create QuickLists if you want to only add a simple list but this will only remind 24 hours, we will delete this list for you!

To create a QuickList just click in the left menu the option QuickList and then just add your name list, the elements and that's it!!

You can also keep your information more than 24 hours just creating a regular list by using the create option in the left menu
